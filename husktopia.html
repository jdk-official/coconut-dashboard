Arrival Day

How Husk Came to Rule in Paradise

The precise circumstances of Husk's arrival on the island remain officially classified. What is known, and celebrated annually on Arrival Day, is that he emerged from the sea fully equipped with a badge, a revolver, and an unshakeable conviction that this island needed a leader.

"I didn't choose the island. The island chose me. Also the current."

Within three days, Husk had named the territory, established a coat of arms (a flower on a hat), and declared the local palm tree his official deputy. Within a week, he had written the constitution on what historians believe was the back of a coconut.

Today, Husktopia is recognised by Husk and several people he has told about it. It is a place of profound calm, warm water, and the firm belief that most problems can be solved by sitting down and watching the sea for a while.

Sunset Watch (Mandatory)

The Constitution

Official Laws of
Husktopia

Ratified by Husk on Day 7. Amendments are considered based on whether Husk is in the mood.

I

No Mondays

The day previously known as Monday has been officially reclassified as "a second Sunday, but quieter."

II

The Sunset Decree

All activity shall cease at sunset. This is not negotiable. It is, however, pleasant.

III

Coconut Protocol

All coconuts belong to the nation. Citizens may borrow them. The nation is very relaxed about this.

IV

Flower Protection Act

The Huskbloom may not be picked, sold, or worn — unless you are Husk, in which case it is traditional.

V

Noise Ordinance

The maximum permissible volume on Husktopia is "the sound of light waves." Anything louder requires a presidential permit.

VI

Visitors Act

Visitors are welcome. Those who attempt to find Husktopia on a map will be gently discouraged, as no such map exists.

VII

The Nap Clause

Citizens have the inalienable right to a post-lunch nap. Duration is unspecified. It ends when it ends.

VIII

On Urgency

Nothing on Husktopia is urgent. If something feels urgent, sit by the water for ten minutes and reassess. Usually resolves itself.

A Message from
the President

"Let it be known that Husktopia is open to all who find their way here, and not findable by those who are trying too hard. The ocean knows. The island knows. Relax."

— President Husk · Husktopia · Year 4 of the Founding

Husk issues periodic decrees from the Presidential Hut. They are posted on the palm tree by the lagoon and occasionally repeated at sunset to anyone nearby.

Decrees are considered law upon utterance. They are also considered optional if Husk has forgotten he said them, which happens.

There is no appeals process. There is, however, a coconut waiting for you on the porch, and the sea is doing something rather lovely at the moment.
